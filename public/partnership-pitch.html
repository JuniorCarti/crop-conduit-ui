Partnership Brief

The Challenge

Smallholder farmers face challenges in unpredictable weather patterns, lack of real-time market information, poor timing of planting and harvesting, and limited access to data-driven advisory tools. These challenges lead to low productivity, post-harvest losses, and reduced income.

Our Solution – AgriSmart

AgriSmart is a digital platform that provides localized climate and weather insights, real-time market price intelligence, data-driven farming recommendations, and decision support tools for farmers. The platform enables farmers to make smarter, timely, and more profitable decisions.
